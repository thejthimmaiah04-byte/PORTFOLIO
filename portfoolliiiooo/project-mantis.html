01 / 05

Inspiration

I wanted to capture the beauty of Empusa pennata — a mantis most people will never see closely enough to appreciate.

I already knew I wanted to model a mantis — I just hadn’t found the right one yet. While I was going through reference photos, I came across Empusa pennata and stopped looking any further. It was so much more beautiful and strange than anything else I’d found that a photograph stopped being enough. That’s when it became a project to model and animate, not just model.
